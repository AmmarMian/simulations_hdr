HDR Dissertation

Experiments

Four chapters spanning the theoretical foundations and practical algorithms for covariance estimation and detection in high-dimensional radar data.

01
Chapter 1
Context
Signal models for SAR and airborne radar. Elliptical distributions, scatter matrix estimation, and the statistical framework underpinning the rest of the work. Why the Gaussian model breaks down — and what to use instead.
SAR Signal models Elliptical distributions Scatter estimation
02
Chapter 2
Detection
Hypothesis testing under structured covariance models. The Generalised Likelihood Ratio Test for Kronecker-structured scatter matrices, online estimation algorithms, and Monte-Carlo power-curve analysis across sample-size regimes.
GLRT Kronecker model Online estimation Power curves Monte Carlo
03
Chapter 3
Machine Learning
Riemannian geometry of the HPD manifold as a substrate for learning. M-estimators as geometry-aware features, geodesic distances, and tangent-space projections for classification and clustering of covariance data.
Riemannian geometry HPD manifold M-estimators Classification
04
Chapter 4
Deep Learning
Algorithm unrolling and geometry-aware neural architectures for covariance estimation. Learning iteration counts, step sizes, and regularisation schedules directly from data — while respecting the manifold structure of HPD matrices.
Algorithm unrolling Deep unfolding Geometric networks HPD-valued layers
